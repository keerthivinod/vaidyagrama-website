Vaidya AR Ramadas

Medical Director

Ayurveda Psychiatry Ophthalmology Nethra Chikitsa
Book Online Book In-Person

Vaidya AR Ramadas hails from Thrissur, Kerala, and comes from a long line of Ayurveda practitioners who have been serving the community for more than half a century. He completed his Ayurveda graduation and went on to earn an M.S. in Psychotherapy and Counseling from IBMS.

He received specialized training in Nethra Chikitsa (Ayurvedic Ophthalmology) under the guidance of the renowned Manakunnath Gopalan Vaidya, and further trained under the celebrated pediatrician C.N.S Gangadharan Vaidyar.

Vaidya Ramadas has served as Medical Director at Vaidyagrama since 2008. Prior to this, he headed Rajendra Pharmacy and R.P.M Ayurvedic Nursing Home. He also directed the Punarnava Ayurveda center in Vietnam, bringing classical Ayurveda to an international audience. He is a chosen Guru of Rashtriya Ayurveda Vidyapeeth.

Book Online Book In-Person

Vaidya Harikrishnan KK comes from a distinguished Ayurvedic lineage rooted in Kerala. He inherited his practice from his father, Vaidyan K.K. Kumaran, and further honed his skills under the renowned Bala Vaidyan Sri Gangadharan.

He completed his graduation in Ayurveda Medicine in 1991 from Ayurveda College, Coimbatore, and has since accumulated over 30 years of dedicated clinical practice.

Vaidya Harikrishnan serves as Chief Physician at Arogyodayam Ayurveda Hospital and as Medical Director at Vaidyagrama. He has expanded the Arogyodayam Ayurveda Vaidyasala into multiple branches, bringing classical Ayurvedic care to a wider community.

Vaidya Ramanandan EK

Medical Director

Neurological Disorders Diabetes Gynecology & Fertility Mental Health Arthritis Spinal Disorders
Book Online Book In-Person

Vaidya Ramanandan EK is a third-generation Ayurveda physician, carrying forward the healing traditions of Kerala's "Erattapalliyalil" and "Thalappully" Vaidya families. He is an alumnus of Ayurveda College, Coimbatore, and also holds an MBA in Hospital Administration from TNOU, Chennai.

His clinical expertise spans a wide range of conditions including neurological disorders, lifestyle diseases, diabetes, gynecology and fertility, mental health, arthritis, gastrointestinal issues, spinal disorders, sinusitis, migraine, and psoriasis. He trained under his father Dr. E.K. Vidyananandan, as well as Sri C. Narayanan Vaidyar and Dr. Krishna Mohan.

As Medical Director at Vaidyagrama and Chief Physician of Thulasi Ayurveda Chikitsalayam, Vaidya Ramanandan has been a driving force in promoting classical Ayurveda. He has organized five international Ayurveda conferences and serves as Zonal President of the Ayurveda Medical Association of India.

Vaidya Omprakash G

Medical Director

Skin & Allergy Treatment Herbal Medicine
Book Online Book In-Person

Described as "a force to be reckoned with in the world of Ayurveda," Vaidya Omprakash G hails from Thanjavur in South India. He completed his BAMS from Coimbatore Ayurveda College in 2009, followed by a Clinical Orientation program at FRLHT, Bengaluru. He has also pursued Sanskrit studies through Sanskrita Bharati, deepening his connection to the classical texts.

Vaidya Omprakash spent seven years at Vaidyagrama, building his expertise in skin and allergy treatment as well as herbal medicine. From 2016 to 2023, he served as Assistant Medical Officer near Kanyakumari, gaining extensive experience with diverse patient populations and conducting herbal field surveys across the region.

He returned to Vaidyagrama in 2023, bringing renewed depth to his practice. An avid nature walker and advocate of forest bathing, Vaidya Omprakash brings a holistic perspective that extends beyond the consultation room.
