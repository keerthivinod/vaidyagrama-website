# PHOTO SPECIFICATIONS GUIDE

> Please read this before submitting any photos. Photos that are too small or in the wrong format will delay the website launch.

---

## Minimum Requirements for All Photos

| Property | Requirement |
|----------|-------------|
| File format | JPG or PNG preferred. WebP also accepted. |
| Minimum width | 1200 pixels wide (1920px preferred for hero/banners) |
| File size | Under 10 MB per photo is ideal |
| Orientation | Landscape (horizontal) for banners. Portrait (vertical) for doctor photos. |
| Resolution | 72 DPI minimum for web. 300 DPI if you have it. |

---

## Photo Categories & Specific Requirements

### Doctor Photos
- **Size:** Minimum 800×800 pixels (square or portrait)
- **Background:** White, off-white, or natural/nature backdrop
- **Framing:** Head and shoulders or full bust (not full body)
- **Expression:** Warm, professional, approachable smile
- **Lighting:** Well-lit face — avoid harsh shadows or backlit photos
- **Clothing:** Professional attire (uniform, white coat, or traditional Indian medical dress)
- **Number needed:** 1 photo per doctor (6 total)
- **Name photos clearly:** e.g., `doctor-ramadas.jpg`, `doctor-ramanandan.jpg`

### Hero / Homepage Banner
- **Size:** Minimum 1920×1080 pixels (Full HD)
- **Subject:** Campus exterior, garden, treatment room, or serene nature scene
- **Must feel:** Peaceful, premium, authentic Ayurvedic
- **Avoid:** People looking directly at camera, cluttered backgrounds, night photos

### Campus / Gallery Photos
- **Size:** Minimum 1200×800 pixels
- **Subject:** Treatment rooms, gardens, campus buildings, dining area, yoga space
- **Number needed:** 8–12 photos total
- **Lighting:** Natural daylight preferred

### Room Photos
- **Size:** Minimum 1200×900 pixels
- **Subject:** Each room type — must show the full room clearly
- **Styling:** Rooms should be clean, tidy, and welcoming before photographing
- **Number needed:** 1–3 photos per room type

### Affiliate Location Photos
- **Size:** Minimum 1000×700 pixels
- **Subject:** Exterior or interior of the affiliate centre/clinic
- **Number needed:** 1 photo per location (5 total)

### Patient Testimonial Headshots
- **Size:** Minimum 200×200 pixels (small is fine for headshots)
- **Background:** Any clean background is fine
- **Note:** Only use photos where the patient has given written consent
