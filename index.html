Frequently Asked Questions

Panchakarma is the classical Ayurvedic five-fold purification and rejuvenation therapy. The five core procedures — Vamana, Virechana, Basti, Nasya, and Raktamokshana — are selected based on your individual constitution (Prakriti) and imbalance (Vikriti). At Vaidyagrama, programs range from 2 to 8 weeks depending on your health condition and goals. Your physician will prescribe the duration after an initial consultation.

Yes — our AyurSakthi Cancer Support Program offers integrative Ayurvedic care for patients undergoing chemotherapy, radiation, post-surgery recovery, and remission maintenance. This is complementary care — it works alongside your oncologist's treatment, not as a replacement. Our physicians are experienced in supporting cancer patients holistically. Please bring your medical reports when you consult with us.

Yes. Our senior physicians offer online consultations via Google Meet, Zoom, Skype, and WhatsApp. You can book an initial or follow-up consultation directly through our booking page. We also offer a free 15-minute introductory chat on Google Meet for those who are not yet sure if Vaidyagrama is the right fit.

The free 15-minute chat is an informal conversation to help you understand if Vaidyagrama is a good fit for your situation. No diagnosis is given, no medical advice is provided. It is a no-pressure orientation session. A paid consultation (30 minutes) is a formal clinical interaction where our physician reviews your case, examines reports, and gives personalised guidance or a treatment prescription.

Absolutely. Vaidyagrama has welcomed guests from over 30 countries. We have international affiliates in Italy, UK, and Vietnam, and our team is experienced in guiding international patients through every step — from the initial online consultation to arrival support. Visit our International Patients page for full details.

Our doctors speak English, Tamil, Malayalam, Hindi, and Sanskrit. Translation support is available for select languages — please contact us in advance.
