How It Works

Your Journey as an International Patient

1

Free Online Consultation

Start with a 15-minute free chat via Google Meet with one of our senior physicians. Share your health history. We assess whether Ayurveda and Vaidyagrama are the right fit for your needs — no pressure, no diagnosis.

2

Program Designed Remotely

After your initial paid consultation, your physician designs your personalized Panchakarma protocol — including duration, treatment types, diet, and medicines. You receive this before you travel so you know exactly what to expect.

3

Travel & Arrival Support

Share your travel itinerary with us and we arrange your airport pickup. We provide a pre-arrival checklist, visa support letter (if required), and detailed directions to our campus in Coimbatore.

4

Treatment Begins

On Day 1, you meet your physician in person for a full clinical assessment. Your personalised treatment program begins. Daily consultations, treatments, meals, and spiritual practices are all included in your stay.

5

Post-Return Follow-Up

After you return home, your physician continues to guide you via online consultations. Medicine refills can be shipped internationally. Follow-ups are scheduled at 30, 90, and 180 days post-treatment.

Visa Support

We assist international patients with medical visa recommendation letters and travel planning. Contact us after your initial consultation for personalized travel guidance. Our team will help coordinate airport transfers (Coimbatore Airport: 34 km, ~1 hour, $20-40).

Languages

Our senior medical team communicates in English. Consultations and all written communications are in English.

Contact our international patient coordinator at +91 4222 364200 or through our contact form. We speak English, Tamil, Malayalam, and Hindi.

Payment & Insurance

International patients may pay in INR, USD, or EUR. Exchange-rate-adjusted pricing is available on request.

Daily costs range from US$100 to US$180/day, covering accommodation, consultations, medications, meals, and therapies. We accept VISA, MasterCard, PayPal, Stripe, Wise, bank transfer, and physical currencies (INR, USD, EUR, AUD, GBP). An advance of USD 1,000 confirms booking.
