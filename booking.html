Before Your Session

How to Prepare for Your Online Consultation

Have your health history and recent reports ready (blood tests, scans, prescriptions)

Join 5 minutes early with a working camera and microphone

Choose a quiet, private space with good lighting on your face

Be prepared to show your tongue — this is a key Ayurvedic diagnostic tool

Have a pen and paper to note the doctor's prescription and recommendations

All health information shared is 100% confidential
