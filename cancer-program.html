Important Medical Disclaimer

Vaidyagrama's cancer support program is integrative and complementary. It works alongside — not instead of — conventional oncology treatment. Ayurveda does not claim to cure cancer. This program is designed to support your body, strengthen immunity, manage treatment side effects, and restore vitality during and after conventional cancer treatment.

Please consult your oncologist before beginning any complementary program. Always share your Ayurvedic treatment plan with your oncologist, and share your oncology treatment details with our physicians.

Our Approach

An Honest, Compassionate Framework

Ayurveda does not promise to cure cancer. What it offers is something equally profound: a scientifically-grounded, time-tested framework to support the body, strengthen immunity, manage the side effects of chemotherapy and radiation, and restore the patient's vitality and sense of self.

At Vaidyagrama, we work in true integration — our physicians communicate with your oncology team, review your treatment records, and design a complementary Ayurvedic protocol that is safe, appropriate, and deeply supportive.

We also recognise that cancer is not only a physical experience. The mental and spiritual dimensions of the journey are equally real — and equally deserving of support.

Our Support Framework

Four Pillars of Support

1. Detoxification

Classical Panchakarma-based detoxification protocols — carefully modified for the patient's current condition and tolerance — help clear accumulated toxins and create a cleaner internal environment for healing.

2. Immunity

Rasayana (rejuvenation) therapies and formulations to strengthen Ojas — the body's innate immunity and vitality. Especially important for patients whose immune systems are compromised by chemotherapy or surgery.

3. Side Effects

Targeted Ayurvedic interventions to help manage the side effects of chemotherapy, radiation, and surgery — including nausea, fatigue, hair loss, neuropathy, digestive disturbances, and pain.

4. Mind & Spirit

Yoga, pranayama, meditation, chanting, and one-on-one counselling provide a container of support for the inner healing process — honouring the psychological and spiritual dimensions of the cancer journey.

Clinical Protocol

The Protocol

The Ayurvedic Lens

Through the Ayurvedic lens, cancer is understood as a systemic imbalance. Disease represents accumulated toxins (ama) that have disrupted the body's natural intelligence. Rather than containment alone, the AyurSakthi program addresses root causes — restoring balance at the deepest level.

Each patient receives a personalized assessment. From this, a tailored treatment protocol is designed incorporating:

  • Lifestyle modifications aligned to your constitution
  • Herbal remedies — safe, traditional preparations
  • Dietary adjustments to support healing
  • Detoxification therapies (modified Panchakarma)
  • Mind-body techniques including yoga, pranayama, and meditation

"This program complements — it does not replace — your oncologist's treatment plan."
